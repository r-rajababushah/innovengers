Himachal Pradesh

 Shimla, alternatively known as Simla, is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and a major tourist destination in India that boasts of a colonial past. The former summer capital for British India, this hill station attracts tourists because of its pleasant weather (especially from March to June), greenery, colonial infrastructure, adventure sports and more. Holidify’s numerous Shimla tour packages are budget-friendly and ideal for travelling with friends or family.

Sikkim

 “Wonders do come in small packages!”, and if you want to witness this, you must visit the picturesque state of Sikkim. Tucked away in the lap of the magical Himalayas, though it is the second smallest state in the country, its prismatic beauty and mesmeric charm will make you wonder!

Uttrakhand

 Uttarakhand is where you can take a break from the hustle and bustle of city life and breathe in the fresh air of the mountains. It also has many hidden gems that are waiting to be discovered. You can find some of the most beautiful trekking trails, waterfalls, and lakes here. The state’s culture is also unique, with a mix of Hindi, Garhwali, and Kumaoni languages and various music and dance forms.
 
Tourist Places
  • The many temples, forts, palaces and monuments in the country make up the historical places of India. These reflect the splendid heritage and culture of the country. The historical places in India depict the stunning craftsmanship on the stone which can be seen in many temples and forts. The Taj Mahal, Red Fort, Humayun's Tomb are famous historical monuments of the Mughal period. Champaner, Dholavira and Nalanda are ancient archaeological sites. Glimpses of colonial architecture can be seen in cities such as Goa, Kolkata and Pondicherry. The Great Living Chola Temples are another example of India's rich history. Konark, Mysore, Orchha, Hyderabad, Bijapur and Lucknow are few other cities that are of historical significance.
  • Popularly referred to as the "Paradise on Earth," Kashmir is like a dream come true for travelers. Nestled in the Himalayas, Kashmir has multiple reasons to be on your India travel bucket list. Getting there is easy with flight connectivity; once you're in, the magic begins. In winter, the place turns into a snowy wonderland.
  • Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is situated in the Kashmir Valley and is often called the “Venice of the East”. Heaven for nature lovers, Srinagar makes for an excellent holiday destination owing to its picturesque landscape featuring lush green gardens, lakes and shikaras, palaces, heritage sites, etc. Enjoy the best of the city with Holidify’s multitude of Srinagar tour packages that are ideal for everyone.
  • Ladakh Tourism: Known as the coldest desert in the world, Ladakh is home to massive mountains, undulating terrains, spectacular gompas, prayer flags and is known for its adventure sports. Ideal for a fun trip, Holidify brings numerous Ladakh tour packages curated to suit all travellers.
  • Gangtok is the capital of Sikkim and is part of the Eastern Himalayan ranges. It is popular as a tourist destination owing to its lush greenery, spectacular views of the surrounding mountain ranges, trekking trails, gushing waterfalls and of course, its association with Buddhism. Gangtok is an ideal destination for a tranquil holiday, one that you can explore with Holidify’s amazing Gangtok tour packages suitable for all kinds of travellers.
  • Manali, nestled in the heart of the Himalayas. Over the course of five days, explore the enchanting beauty of Manali and its surrounding areas, including the iconic Solang Valley, the spiritual town of Manikaran, and the picturesque town of Kullu. Indulge in thrilling adventure activities, visit ancient temples, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of this Himalayan paradise.
  • Picturesque landscapes and vibrant cultures of Northeast India, starting from Guwahati and culminating in the charming city of Shillong. Explore the pristine beauty of Meghalaya's rolling hills, crystal-clear rivers, and lush greenery, while immersing yourself in this enchanting region's rich history and traditions.
